We are excited that you've booked your {{ package_type }} journey with us! To ensure you are fully prepared for this blessed experience, please review the checklist below with important preparation tips, travel essentials, and required vaccinations.

Preparation Tips for Your Journey:

  • Ensure all travel documents are in order: passport, visa, and flight details.
  • Dress comfortably for hot weather; light, breathable clothes are essential. Don’t forget comfortable walking shoes.
  • Pack a small first-aid kit with medications, hand sanitizers, and personal hygiene products.
  • Carry a portable charger and adapters for your electronics.

Travel Essentials:

  • Travel insurance (make sure it covers health, travel disruptions, and medical needs).
  • Copies of your important documents (passport, visa, medical records) in case of loss or theft.
  • Comfortable shoes are crucial as you will be walking extensively.
  • A reusable water bottle is important to stay hydrated.

Required Vaccinations:

  • Meningococcal Vaccine: A mandatory vaccine for all pilgrims.
  • Flu Vaccine: Strongly recommended for all travelers.
  • COVID-19 Vaccination: Ensure that you meet the vaccination requirements set by the authorities.
  • Polio Vaccine: This may be required based on your country of origin.

For the latest updates or additional health advice, please consult your healthcare provider or visit the official Hajj/Umrah website.
